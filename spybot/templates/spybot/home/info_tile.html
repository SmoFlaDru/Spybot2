Spybot 2 release

Benno and Justus rewrote the OG teamspeak spybot as the glorious Spybot 2. It is now written in Python as opposed to PHP and more modern, maintainable and beautiful. In addition, Spybot 2 has more charts. Btw, did you know Spybot 2 supports dark mode? Try the toggle in the top right.
